Our Story

Passion Plated,
Perfection Served

Born from a lifelong love of food and culture, Urban Spice opened its doors in 2014 with a single mission: to redefine the modern dining experience. We believe that every meal should be a memory — a conversation between the chef's vision and the guest's senses.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Marco Bellini, a James Beard Award finalist whose career spans Michelin-starred kitchens in Milan, Paris, and Tokyo. Marco brings an obsessive dedication to ingredient sourcing, drawing from local farms and artisan producers to craft dishes that feel both familiar and revelatory.
